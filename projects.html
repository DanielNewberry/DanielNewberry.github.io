Projects

Some of the projects I’ve been involved with over the years.

Ongoing

  • Exploring Artistic Workspace in Newcastle: A series of conversations with artists aimed at understanding how access, or the lack of it, to quality artistic spaces impacts their practice and wellbeing.
  • More Than Meanwhile Spaces: Led by Dr Emma Coffield, this collaborative project explores long-term futures for artist-led spaces and workspaces in the North East of England.
  • PhD Research: A qualitative study exploring sustainable entrepreneurship within the North East of England, with the main research objective being to critically analyse the centrality of the owner-manager in green micro-businesses.

Archive

  • Making the Clayton Street Corridor: Academics from Newcastle University worked to support grassroots artist involvement with the new Cultural and Creative Zone (CCZ) in Newcastle city centre. Funded by Newcastle City Council.
  • MA Dissertation: A quantitative study exploring how environmental self-identity influences the pro-environmental behaviour of the self-employed in the North East's creative industries.
