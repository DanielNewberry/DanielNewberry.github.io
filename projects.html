Ongoing

	---

	Nature for People and Place in the Wansbeck Catchment:
	A community-led project enhancing nature recovery along the Wansbeck catchment. Led by NICRE, the initial phase involves stakeholder mapping, data review, and community engagement to connect people, nature, and place.

	PhD Research:
	A qualitative study exploring sustainable entrepreneurship within the North East of England, with the main research objective being to critically analyse the centrality of the owner-manager in green micro-businesses.



	Archive

	---

	Exploring Artistic Workspace in Newcastle:
	A series of conversations with artists aimed at understanding how access, or the lack of it, to quality artistic spaces impacts their practice and wellbeing.
	
	More Than Meanwhile Spaces:
	Led by Dr Emma Coffield, this collaborative project explores long-term futures for artist-led spaces and workspaces in the North East of England.

	Making the Clayton Street Corridor:
	Academics from Newcastle University worked to support grassroots artist involvement with the new Cultural and Creative Zone (CCZ) in Newcastle city centre. Funded by Newcastle City Council.

	MA Dissertation:
	A quantitative study exploring how environmental self-identity influences the pro-environmental behaviour of the self-employed in the North East's creative industries.
